About Me

Hello, I'm Paul Larison. I recently completed a Master's degree in Data Science, and I am currently progressing through Google's Machine Learning Engineer Learning Path to fortify my skills. My goal is to bring a well-rounded perspective to enterprise-level projects, leveraging the latest advancements in machine learning to drive impactful business outcomes.

You might be wondering why I'm looking for a data science job when I was a perfectly competent sysadmin. The answer is that I'm attracted to complicated rulesets and I'm tired of playing video games. I like tinkering with and optimizing complex systems, whether it's an online game like EvE Online or a tabletop like Shadowrun. Data science, and especially machine learning, allows me to scratch the same itch with two major advantages:

  • It's more complex than any game ever conceived. No matter how good I get, there will always be more to learn and discover.
  • I can do it at work, which makes wasting my life away in front of a screen a lot less tempting when I have free time.
